1519330 - Missing Authorization Check in Healthcare Business Partner

**Description**

An authenticated user can exploit the functionality of the Healthcare Business Partner (HBP) without proper authorization checks. This vulnerability can potentially lead to an escalation of privileges, allowing unauthorized access to restricted functionalities within the system.

**Symptom**

- Unauthorized access to restricted functionalities within the Healthcare Business Partner component.
- Potential escalation of privileges due to missing authorization checks.

**Reason and Prerequisites**

The Healthcare Business Partner lacks appropriate permission checks for authenticated users, allowing them to access functionalities without proper authorization. This oversight may result in undesired system behavior and security breaches.

**Solution**

Follow the manual steps provided below to implement the necessary authorization checks:

1. Go to transaction `SE11` and select the **Database Table** radio button.
2. Repeat steps 3 to 8 for each of the following tables:
   - `ISH_NEG_FOODC`
   - `ISH_PREF_FOODC`
   - `ISH_RISKC`
   - `ISH_INSTYPEC`
   - `ISH_MEMBTYPEC`
   - `ISH_RESPTYPEC`
3. In the field **Database Table**, enter the table name listed above.
4. Click on the **Display** button.
5. From the **Utilities** menu, select **Assign Authorization Group**.
6. From the **Table/View** menu, choose **Display â†’ Change**.
7. In the **Authorization** column associated with the corresponding table, change the value from `&NC&` to `CRMC`.
8. Save your changes.
