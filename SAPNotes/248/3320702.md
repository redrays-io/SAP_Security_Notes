3320702 - [CVE-2023-36917] Password Change rate limit bypass in SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform

### Symptom
SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform allows an unauthorized attacker who has hijacked a user session to bypass the victim’s old password via brute force, due to an unrestricted rate limit for the password change functionality. Although the attack has no impact on integrity loss or system availability, it could lead to an attacker completely taking over a victim’s account.

### Other Terms
- **Information Exposure**
- **Information Leak**
- [CVE-2023-36917](https://www.cve.org/CVERecord?id=CVE-2023-36917)

### Reason and Prerequisites
An attacker needs to hijack the server session.

### Solution
Rate limitation has been applied to the change password request.

For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy, see the Knowledge Base Article [2144559](https://me.sap.com/notes/2144559) in the References section.
