#### Other Terms
Authorization, authorization check, BC-CUS-TOL-HMT

#### Reason and Prerequisites
BC-CUS-TOL-HMT does not contain authorization checks for verifying an authenticated user's authorization to access certain functions. This omission may result in undesired system behavior.

**Prerequisites:**  
- [Note 1971238](https://me.sap.com/notes/1971238)  
- [Note 2017050](https://me.sap.com/notes/2017050)
