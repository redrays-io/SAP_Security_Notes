3073325 - [CVE-2021-33700] Missing Authentication check in SAP Business One

**Symptom**

SAP Business One allows a local attacker with access to the victim's browser under certain circumstances to log in as the victim without knowing their password. The attacker could obtain highly sensitive information and take substantial control of the vulnerable application.

**Other Terms**

Broken Authentication, [CVE-2021-33700](https://cve.mitre.org/cgi-bin/cvename.cgi?name=CVE-2021-33700)

**Solution**

Disable the related vulnerability function. Customers need to implement or upgrade to SAP Business One 10.0 PL2105 Hotfix1.

The patches can be found on the [SAP ONE Support Launchpad](https://launchpad.support.sap.com/#/softwarecenter).

**Workaround**

Please assess the workaround applicability for your SAP landscape prior to implementation.

Note that this workaround is a temporary fix and is not a permanent solution. SAP strongly recommends you to apply the corrections outlined in the security note, which can be done in lieu of the workaround or after the workaround is implemented.

*Don't use the lockout function and set the timeout to max value (admin user: 999 minutes, normal user: 30 minutes).*
